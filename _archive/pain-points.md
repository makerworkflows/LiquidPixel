# The Digital Uproar - Customer Pain Points

## Virtual Tour & Venue Representation

1. **Limited tour capacity** -- You can only give so many physical/personal tours in a given work day. Time is the bottleneck.

2. **Uncertain book rate from in-person tours** -- Giving someone a tour and hearing back is never certain. But when someone goes through a virtual tour and *still* calls back, that's a strong booking signal. Virtual pre-qualifies serious buyers.

3. **Static photos don't do a place justice** -- Flat images fail to convey the feel, scale, and flow of a venue.

4. **Phone + PDF limitations** -- Not able to give a proper representation of a venue over the phone with just PDFs and photos. The medium kills the message.
