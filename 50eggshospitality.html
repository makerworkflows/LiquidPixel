Case Study

50 Eggs Hospitality

How we helped 50 Eggs Hospitality increase private dining bookings by 18% and achieve 2.5x longer page engagement with immersive virtual tours of their flagship restaurants.

The Client

About 50 Eggs Hospitality

50 Eggs Hospitality is a Miami-based restaurant group behind acclaimed brands including Yardbird Southern Table & Bar, Chicago Cut Steakhouse, Spring Chicken, and Chica. With locations spanning Miami, Las Vegas, Chicago, Dallas, and beyond, they're known for elevated dining experiences that blend culinary excellence with vibrant atmosphere.

Liquid Pixel works with 50 Eggs as a trusted virtual tour partner, creating immersive digital experiences for their private dining and event spaces.

The Challenge

Private Dining Rooms Nobody Could See

Private dining spaces at Chicago Cut and Yardbird were consistently under-booked. The physical separation from the main restaurant floor made these premium spaces difficult to showcase through traditional marketing materials.

PDF brochures and static photo galleries couldn't convey the ambiance, scale, or exclusivity of the private rooms. Event planners and corporate clients needed to physically visit before committing -- a friction point that killed conversion rates.

50 Eggs needed a way to bring the private dining experience directly to their prospects' screens.

Our Solution

Menus Meet Immersion

We delivered high-definition virtual tours of the private dining rooms at Chicago Cut and Yardbird, integrated directly into their event booking platform.

Each tour features embedded interactive menus that let planners browse prix fixe options while standing (virtually) inside the room. Custom hotspots detail seating configurations, A/V setup, and capacity for each space.

The result: prospects explore the space, browse the menu, and submit an inquiry -- all without leaving the page.

Results

The Impact

2.5x Time on Page

Visitors exploring the virtual tour spent 2.5 times longer on private dining pages compared to the previous static gallery format.

+18% Increase in Private Bookings

Private dining inquiries and confirmed bookings increased by 18% within the first quarter of launching the virtual tour integration.
